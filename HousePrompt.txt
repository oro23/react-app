Design a modern and elegant 2-story residential house layout for a 2 kanal plot, divided into two connected but independent 1 kanal houses.

Plot Details:

Each kanal: 55 ft (width) × 99 ft (depth)
Combined layout: 110 ft × 99 ft, arranged in a straight row
The design should appear harmonious and symmetrical, yet allow clear separation between the two houses

Design Requirements:

Each 1 kanal unit should function as a fully independent house
Use a modern architectural style with clean lines, large windows, and a premium, elegant finish
Houses should be visually connected but with distinct entrances and privacy

Veranda Specifications:

Include a front veranda in each house, positioned in front of the main hall/living area
The veranda roof should be 3 feet lower than the main roof level
Install a decorative grill/pergola structure on the veranda roof
Add a separate grill/pergola design for the main roof, distinct from the veranda grill

Layout Suggestions (per house):

Ground floor: entrance lobby, drawing room, living hall, kitchen, dining, guest bedroom, powder room
First floor: 3–4 bedrooms with attached bathrooms, family lounge, terrace/balcony access
Include natural light, ventilation, and functional circulation

Additional Preferences:

Provide parking space for each house
Use modern materials (glass, stone, wood accents, neutral tones)
Consider landscaping in front setback
Maintain privacy between both houses while keeping a unified facade

Output Expectations:

2D floor plans (both floors for each house)
Front elevation showing both houses together
Highlight veranda level difference and grill designs clearly
